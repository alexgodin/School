This article is about a decrease in the trade deficit. Our trade deficit is the amount by which our imports exceed our exports. The way we decreased our deficit was by increasing the amount we export. The article also discusses the fact that the number of people seeking unemployment benefits rose. These two statistics conflict because one is a positive economic indicator while the other is a negative indicator.
